New American American cuisine. The best wine selection in Cleveland.

A perennial winner of accolades and awards, Culinary Coders is known for creative dining and the best wine selection in the region. Named one of Open Table’s “Best Wine List in the Greater Cleveland area (Sept. 2016),” with the “Best Wine List” in the Ohio region (Open Table, Sept. 2016) and a “must-visit restaurant” by The Plain Dealer. Culinary Coders has reached to exceed the highest standards in food, beverage and service every day. Our premium dinners and elegant ambience attract patrons from throughout Cleveland, Pittsburgh, Summit County and Hamilton County for celebrations, business meetings and private dining events.

AWARD-WINNING WINE LIST

Our wine selections include more than 450 premium bottled wines and 40 wines by the glass. Special Coravin selections, curated flights and create-your-own flights with our exclusive 1.5, 3 and 5 oz pours.

PRIVATE DINING

Culinary Codes offer closed-door private dining spaces for up to 70 guests for parties, receptions, lunch and dinner meetings.

DELECTABLE DESSERTS

All of our imaginative desserts are created in-house! Some of our favorites are goat cheese gelato (a different flavor daily), shortbread cookies with lemon curd and figs.
